Support

Last updated July 22, 2026

Stuck on something? Most questions are answered below; for anything else, email support@vortas.io and a human will get back to you.

The app asks for location — why, and which setting?

Location is how runs are tracked and treasures are placed on paths near you. Choose "Allow While Using App" at minimum; enabling background location keeps tracking accurate when the screen is off mid-run. Location is processed on your phone and never sent to us — see the privacy policy.

My distance or pace looks wrong

  • GPS needs open sky to settle — the first minute of a run is the least accurate.
  • Phones in a deep pocket or armband do fine; a bag pressed against your body can degrade the signal.
  • Under bridges or between tall buildings, Cologra estimates short gaps from your step count and your own stride length, clearly bounded so it can never inflate a run.
  • Runs that never leave the start (under ~100 m) are discarded on purpose — accidental taps don't pollute history.

I don't hear the coach or treasure pings

  • Check Settings → Audio in the app: voice can be set to full, key moments only, or off.
  • Cues duck your music briefly rather than pausing it; if you hear nothing, check the phone's media volume (not just ringer).
  • Voice guidance speaks the app's language — if your language pack isn't installed on the phone, the app will tell you in Settings → Language.

Backups: moving to a new phone

Settings → Backup → Export backup file saves everything (runs, treasures, fitness profile) as one file — send it anywhere you like, then Import backup on the new phone. This works across platforms, including Android → iPhone. Optional automatic cloud backup (off by default) keeps that file refreshed in your own iCloud Drive or a folder you choose.

Deleting my data

Everything lives on your phone: delete the app and it's all gone. If you made backups, delete those files from your storage too. There is no account to close — we never had your data.

Reporting a bug

Email support@vortas.io with your phone model, OS version, and what happened just before the problem. Screenshots or a screen recording help a lot.
