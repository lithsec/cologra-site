01 · Sound

The first running game designed for ears, not screens

Every other running app assumes you'll look at it. Cologra assumes you won't — and builds the entire game out of sound.

Spatial treasure pings

Pings pan left and right by true bearing and rise in pitch as you close in. Close your eyes and you can point at the treasure.

A coach that speaks when it matters

Whether a chase is still winnable, when to let one go, when your own stride says you're tiring. No chatter, no filler.

Your music stays yours

Cues duck the volume for a moment and get out of the way. Never paused, never interrupted.

Built for blind runners too

Eyes-free by design means screen-reader and voice-query support isn't an afterthought — it's the same product.

02 · Privacy

Your runs never leave your phone

Not "we take privacy seriously." Architecturally, literally: Cologra has no accounts and runs no servers. Routes, times, fitness — all of it lives on your device and nowhere else. Optional backup goes to your iCloud or a folder you pick, off by default, behind a consent screen that says exactly what's in the file.

Don't take our word for it — decompile the app. A strings scan of the shipped binary finds zero server endpoints, zero API keys, zero trackers, zero ad SDKs. The only network traffic is downloading public map data. Even the coverage map renders entirely on-device.

03 · Honesty

A coach that shows its work

Your fitness model is a Critical Speed fit of your own efforts — sports science older than the smartwatch, computed on your phone. Every headline number has a "why?" that opens the actual reasoning, citations included.

  • No daily-streak guilt. Streaks count weeks here — rest days are training, and daily-run streaks are an injury mechanic. We refuse them.
  • No fake metrics. A phone can't honestly measure ground-contact time or vertical oscillation, so Cologra doesn't pretend to. Cadence and stride length — measured against your own baseline, never a universal number.
  • No made-up scores. No "readiness percentages," no proprietary mystery indexes. If we can't explain a number, we don't show it.

04 · Play

Compete with the only person who matters

Race your ghost

Start from a familiar spot and you're up against your own previous run — "you're 30 meters ahead of last Tuesday's you." No leaderboards, no strangers.

Explore every street

The app knows which paths around you you've never run, tells you where the unexplored ground is, and draws your coverage map — computed entirely on your phone.

Duo runs, no servers

Agree on a code word with a friend and you both hunt the exact same treasures, wherever each of you runs. Compare hauls after.

Own your data

One-tap export of everything you've earned. Works across platforms — even Android to iPhone. Delete the app, and it's genuinely gone.
